the cut & paste orchestra is a suite of software instruments I made from samples recorded by myself & a few special friends

the samples are all raw, dry, and unpredictably imperfect - qualities which lend themselves to authenticity & creativity in the otherwise sterile digital realm

load them up with Kontakt or EXS24 (Logic's native sampler format also supported by Ableton), plug-in your MIDI keyboard, and let the sounds guide u
